Breathe-Safe

Web application for AQI monitoring

Back to Showroom

The Challenge

We check the weather before stepping out.⛅ We check traffic before choosing a route.🚦 But how often do we check the air we are about to breathe? There was no simple way to instantly know how bad the air actually was !!.

The Solution

Developed a full-stack web application for AQI monitoring, which is integrated with a custom-designed IoT-based hardware component named “Travel Buddy” for real-time air quality monitoring. Implemented AI-driven insights and health suggestions to enable users to understand air quality patterns and make informed decisions about the air they breathe.
