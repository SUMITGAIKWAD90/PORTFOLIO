MDM-CONNECT

Blockchain-Powered Meal Scheme Management

Back to Showroom

The Challenge

Managing the Mid Day Meal Scheme requires transparency, secure transactions, and efficient fund management. The challenge was to create a blockchain-based solution that ensures accountability while providing real-time digital payments for schools.

The Solution

I created MDM-Connect, a secure web application for the Mid Day Meal Scheme built on the Polygon network. The app enables real-time digital payments and fund management for schools, with user-friendly dashboards for managing devices, tracking transactions, and providing public access to school funding data for complete transparency.
